The Artist

Meet Shweta Gupta

Internationally certified mehandi artist, trainer to 7,500+ students, and the hands behind the art beyond imagination.

15+years of
artistry

Shweta Gupta picked up her first henna cone as a young girl — and never put it down. Today she is an internationally certified mehandi artist whose work travels from Birgunj's wedding halls to clients across the border, one pair of hands at a time.

In 2011 she opened the doors of Shri Radhe Krishan Mehandi Training Centre with one belief: the art that gave her an identity could give thousands of women theirs. More than 7,500 students later, her classroom is still full — and many of her students now take their own bridal bookings.

Her signature? Photo-to-henna portraits. Hand her a photograph — a wedding picture, a grandmother, a whole family — and she redraws it in henna with a fineness that makes people check the phone twice.
