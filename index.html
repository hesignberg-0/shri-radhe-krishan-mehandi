From Her Side

“Every hand that comes to me carries a story, my henna just makes it visible.”

I still remember my first cone, the smell of fresh henna, the fear of one wrong line, and the joy when the pattern finally bloomed. That joy has never left me. Fifteen years and countless brides later, I sit with the same excitement before every single hand.

In 2011 I opened this training centre with one belief: the art that gave me my identity can give thousands of women theirs. Today, when a student of mine takes her first bridal booking, I celebrate like it is my own. Over 7,500 of them have walked out of here as artists, and that is the design I am most proud of.

Whether you come to me as a bride, for a festival, or with a cone in your hand ready to learn, you are not a client to me. You are the next page of this story.

Shweta Gupta Founder & Lead Artist, Internationally Certified Mehandi Trainer

Good To Know

Questions, answered

How long does the mehandi colour last?

The stain is darkest 24 to 48 hours after application and usually stays beautiful for 1 to 2 weeks. Keep the paste on longer, avoid water for the first few hours, and the colour rewards you.

How early should I book for a wedding?

Wedding season dates fill up fast, message us on WhatsApp as soon as your date is fixed and we will reserve it for you.

Do you come to our home or venue?

Yes, home and venue visits can be arranged. Tell us your location and date on WhatsApp and we will confirm everything.

I have never held a henna cone. Can I still join the training?

Absolutely, most of our 7,500+ students started as complete beginners. The Basic Course begins with cone control and basic strokes, step by step.

Will I get a certificate after the course?

Yes, you receive a certificate on completing your course, and more importantly, the skill and portfolio to start taking your own bookings.

What are the course fees and timings?

Batches and fees vary by course and season. Send the admission form or message us on WhatsApp and we will share the current schedule with you.
