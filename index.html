From Her Side

“Every hand that comes to me carries a story — my henna just makes it visible.”

I still remember my first cone — the smell of fresh henna, the fear of one wrong line, and the joy when the pattern finally bloomed. That joy has never left me. Thirteen years and countless brides later, I sit with the same excitement before every single hand.

In 2011 I opened this training centre with one belief: the art that gave me my identity can give thousands of women theirs. Today, when a student of mine takes her first bridal booking, I celebrate like it is my own. Over 7,000 of them have walked out of here as artists — that is the design I am most proud of.

Whether you come to me as a bride, for a festival, or with a cone in your hand ready to learn — you are not a client to me. You are the next page of this story.

— Shweta Gupta Founder & Lead Artist, Internationally Certified Mehandi Trainer
